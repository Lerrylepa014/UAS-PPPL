Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my financial data with FinFlow?

We use bank-level security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and comply with PCI DSS standards. Your data is stored in secure, encrypted databases with regular security audits.

What are your pricing plans and fees?

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Our basic plan is free, premium starts at $9.99/month, and enterprise solutions are custom-priced. Transfer fees start at $2.99 for domestic and vary for international transfers.

How long does API integration take?

Most integrations can be completed within 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Our technical team provides comprehensive documentation, SDKs, and dedicated support throughout the integration process.

What support do you provide?

We offer 24/7 technical support, comprehensive documentation, live chat, email support, and dedicated account managers for enterprise clients. Premium users get priority support with faster response times.

Do you support international transactions?

Yes, we support transactions in over 50 countries and 25+ currencies. Our global network ensures fast, secure international transfers with competitive exchange rates and real-time tracking.

Are you compliant with financial regulations?

Absolutely. We maintain compliance with all relevant financial regulations including KYC/AML requirements, GDPR, PCI DSS, and are licensed in all jurisdictions where we operate.
